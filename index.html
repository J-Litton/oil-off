Oil-OFF™ – Non-flammable bio-solvent A non-flammable solvent for the breakdown of oil for cleaning processes, from i-SOLV™ Research Laboratories Inc. A revolutionary development in the field of environmentally-friendly chemistry, Oil-OFF™ is the leading all-in-one solution for the safe and effective clean-up of heavy oil from sand, soil, and mud.
While other products may work well to clean heavy oils or soils, Oil-OFF™ conquers both challenges at the same time using powerful ecologically-sensitive chemicals for the most effortless clean-up possible. Whatever your challenge – invert mud, tar sands, or heavy oil mixed with soil – none match the force of Oil-OFF™ bio-solvent.
This breakthrough product combines highly effective surfactants and wetting agents that cut through any medium to make it pliable enough for easy rinsing. Simultaneously, Oil-OFF™ emulsifies heavy oils, including tar and bitumen, using synthetic and organic bio-solvents for rapid, safe removal.
Working with Oil-OFF™ also means a safer jobsite since it inhibits static electric charges, prevents corrosion, has low-toxicity, and eliminates the re-deposition of soils.
Using the latest in green chemistry techniques, Oil-OFF™ is a highly advanced product in i-SOLV™ bio-solvent line, solidifying our commitment to producing industrial chemistry’s most effective and safe surfactant and wetting agents possible.

NEW! Oil-OFF Gel
Oil-OFF now comes in a gel that's easy to spray onto vertical or inverted surfaces or parts.i-SOLV is pleased to announce that Oil-OFF now comes in gel form.
Available in three different thicknesses, this form of the same effective product allows for better coating coverage when sprayed on horizontal or inverted surfaces. This helps avoid both run-off waste and difficult manual applications for hard to reach surfaces and parts.
